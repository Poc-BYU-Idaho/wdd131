Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to be a convenient location to find resistance training exercises and information about how to perform them.

Audience

Anyone who wants to learn about resistance training exercises.

Dynamic elements

At a minimum, the website will allow users to search for exercises and then display them on the page. It will also dynamically generate child pages for each exercise. I may also add the options of choosing a color scheme and searching for exercises by the muscles that they hit.
